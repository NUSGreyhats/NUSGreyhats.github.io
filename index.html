X-CTF

"X-CTF" stands for "Cross Varsity - Capture The Flag" competition. It is a cyber security competition organized annually where teams from the tertiary institutions are welcome to compete for the coveted X-CTF trophy.

Realistic Challenges

Challenges in the competition are designed to be as realistic and similar to real-life scenarios as possible.

CTF 101

Capture The Flag 101 is a capture-the-flag (CTF) style workshop that will be spanned over two Saturdays. Participants will learn techniques and tools to exploit software vulnerabilities to uncover secret flags hidden in various challenge. This workshop is designed to be progressive and is beginner friendly. In addition, we will have a live CTF scoreboard to show your ranking :)

Progressive hands-on workshop

Do not worry, the workshop is designed to allow beginners to follow easily.
